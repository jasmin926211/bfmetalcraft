Since 1960 Bf metal craft Engineering firm helping clients.

Our firm M/s. Kansara Babubhai Fakirchand was established in the year 1960 in the old region of Surat under the above name as joint family organization. This firm was making Utensils, Fancy Presentation Boxes in Brass & Copper material, S.S. Dining Trays for Railways and Defence canteens.

From 1978 onwards we started our another unit at the address above with Industrial Press Componrnts with development of Crio Containers of M/s IBP Engg. Division Sewri, Bombay. This Crio Containers are Aluminium deep draw components of large diameter, Long depth and high thickness of 3mm which were used in Dairy and subjected to be used at extra low temperature of below freezing point. We have also developed many extra deep draw components like Parasute container, Star Container and many other Components for ORDNANCE FACTORY ( DEFENCE ).

Our quality of work and timely completion of jobs have been proved by repeate orders since 1985. We have been Supplying this components to ORDNANCE FACTORY DEHU ROAD, KHADKI AND R&D PASHAN PUNE.
